Research projects & services

We support public health agencies, research teams, and clinical partners with targeted analytic work, from one-off questions to multi-year modeling backbones.

Data harmonization & denominators

Align survey, surveillance, and population data so your comparisons are valid and defensible.

  • BRFSS, YRBSS, census/ACS, and registry linkages
  • County- and sub-state-level denominators and small-area estimates
  • Documentation of variable definitions and assumptions

Modeling & risk stratification

Build models that answer policy-relevant questions while respecting uncertainty and bias.

  • Survey-weighted regression and predictive risk scores
  • Trend and cluster analysis across jurisdictions
  • Scenario modeling for planning and grant applications

Translation & grant support

Convert complex methods into materials that stakeholders and reviewers can quickly understand.

  • Figures, tables, and summary briefs for leadership
  • Methods and analytic sections for grant proposals
  • Reproducible code notebooks and technical appendices

Emerging PAEON lines of work

Acropolis Analytics RI is developing the Population-Adjusted Epidemiology Outcomes Network (PAEON) with modular “Labors” focused on:

  • Border-region chronic disease, infectious disease, and crime burden
  • Cognitive decline and caregiver strain projections
  • Youth risk behaviors and physical activity clustering

These lines can be adapted into collaborative grant proposals or custom analytic projects.
