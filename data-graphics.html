Public-facing resources (coming soon)

Example structure for future uploads

Internally, files will be organized by project. For example:

  • PAEON I – PAX: border-region chronic disease and crime mapping
  • PAEON VII – HYGEIA: youth physical activity and screen-time clusters

Each entry will list available resources (e.g., “brief PDF”, “figure set”, “CSV extract”) with clear notes on version and date.
