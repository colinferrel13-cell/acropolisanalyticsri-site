Data

This page will host public, de-identified data releases and selected outputs from Acropolis Analytics Research Institute projects, starting with PAEON and related caregiver and facility-level work.

Overview

The goal of this section is to make key results and reference tables broadly available without exposing individual-level records. Over time, this will include county-level summary datasets, crosswalks, and visual outputs that partners can reuse in their own planning and reporting.

All data posted here will be aggregated, de-identified, and accompanied by brief documentation describing sources, methods, and version history.

PAEON-Derived Data (In Development)

As the Population-Adjusted Epidemiology Outcomes Network (PAEON) matures, selected outputs will be published here as static reference files.

  • County-level summary tables for chronic disease, screening, and access indicators
  • Border-region extracts from PAEON I — PAX (U.S.–Mexico corridor)
  • Modeled burden and risk summaries from AORTA, PANCREA, PNEUMA, MNEME, and ONKOS
  • Basic crosswalks and code lists to support downstream analyses

Initial releases are expected to focus on stable, widely used indicators and simple tabular formats (for example, CSV files) that can be ingested by most statistical and GIS tools.

Caregiver & Facility-Level Outputs (Planned)

For ADRD caregiver and facility-level projects, Acropolis Analytics Research Institute plans to publish aggregated, de-identified summaries that illustrate patterns in caregiver burden and related outcomes.

  • Summary tables of caregiver characteristics, time and task burden, and reported stress
  • Facility-level or regional aggregates where sample sizes permit safe reporting
  • Selected figures and graphics used in partner reports

Facility-specific or partner-specific datasets will only be shared under appropriate agreements and will not appear here in raw form.

Formats & Documentation

Each data release will include enough context for another analyst to understand what they are looking at and how it was created.

  • Machine-readable files (for example, CSV or similar tabular formats)
  • Short data dictionaries describing variables, units, and any key recodes
  • Methods notes summarizing sources, inclusion criteria, and modeling approaches
  • Version labels and release dates so users can track updates over time

Access & Custom Work

The public data listed here is intended for broad use in planning, research, and communication. If you are interested in custom extracts, facility-specific reports, or additional modeling built on these datasets, Acropolis Analytics Research Institute can often support that work as a separate engagement.

To discuss access, collaboration, or planned future releases, please reach out through the contact form with a brief description of your data needs and how you plan to use the results.
