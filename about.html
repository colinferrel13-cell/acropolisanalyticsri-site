About Acropolis Analytics RI

Acropolis Analytics Research Institute is a single-member research LLC focused on population-adjusted epidemiology, data harmonization, and decision-ready public health analytics.

Approach

We combine classical epidemiologic methods with modern computation and careful attention to denominators. Our work emphasizes reproducible code, transparent assumptions, and clear documentation so that results can withstand scrutiny and be reused across projects.

Founder

Acropolis Analytics RI was founded by Colin Ferrel, MPH, an epidemiologist with experience in:

  • State-level Alzheimer’s & related dementias (ADRD) surveillance
  • Analyzing chronic disease risk and physical activity patterns using BRFSS and YRBSS
  • Designing multi-dataset projects that bridge public health practice and academic research
  • Supporting grant development with methods, power considerations, and analytic plans
