Reports

This section will host selected reports, presentations, and written deliverables from Acropolis Analytics Research Institute projects, as well as publications and prior work by LLC members and collaborators where sharing is permitted.

Overview

The Reports section is intended as a reading library—a place to see how data, models, and field experience come together in written form. Over time, it will include a mix of formal reports, slide decks, and selected manuscripts that illustrate the kinds of questions Acropolis Analytics Research Institute helps answer.

Documents posted here are typically final or near-final versions that can be shared publicly or with broad partner audiences. Internal drafts and confidential materials will not appear in this space.

Project Reports & Briefs

As projects mature, key written outputs will be listed here with short descriptions and links to downloadable files (for example, PDFs or slide decks).

  • Project summaries and technical reports for major analytic engagements
  • Executive summaries and Policy Briefs tailored to leadership audiences
  • Methodological appendices for readers who want additional detail

When available, each entry will note the primary project (for example, PAEON or caregiver surveys), the year, and the intended audience to help readers find what is most relevant to them.

Presentations & Slide Decks

Many findings are first shared in meetings, webinars, or conference settings. This section will collect selected slide decks and presentation materials that can be made available outside those events.

  • Conference talks and invited presentations (where redistribution is allowed)
  • Slides used in briefings to health departments, facilities, and partner organizations
  • Annotated decks that walk through key figures, maps, and conclusions

Presentations may be lightly edited to remove any sensitive or partner-specific details before being posted.

Publications & Prior Work

In addition to LLC-generated reports, this page may highlight peer-reviewed publications and notable deliverables from the past work of Acropolis Analytics Research Institute members, where appropriate.

  • Citations and links to journal articles or book chapters
  • Selected reports and white papers produced under prior roles
  • Outputs from collaborative projects with academic and agency partners

Where possible, entries will include a short note on the role played by Acropolis Analytics Research Institute or its members (for example, lead analyst, co-author, or technical advisor).

Access & Citation

Unless otherwise noted, documents posted here may be cited or shared with appropriate attribution to Acropolis Analytics Research Institute and any listed partners or authors.

  • Standard citation formats will be provided for reports and publications
  • Some documents may be labeled as draft, internal, or limited-distribution
  • If a report is based on restricted data, that will be noted in the description

For questions about reuse, redistribution, or access to underlying data, please reach out through the contact form with the title of the report and how you plan to use it.
