What we focus on

Acropolis Analytics RI is a micro-institute focused on rigorous, transparent epidemiologic analysis. We specialize in harmonizing survey, surveillance, and population data so that trends and risks are directly comparable across time and geography.

Key domains

  • Chronic disease & cardiometabolic burden
  • Cognitive decline and dementia surveillance
  • Youth risk behaviors and physical activity
  • Border health and cross-national comparisons
